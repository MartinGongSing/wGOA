Neph SOP
Version 1: 2017.06.21
Nephelometer calibration
  1. Pump 1 should be running and ModuloCorte should be off
  2. Run NephWin Software
  3. Run -> Calibration
  4. Click 1 to set Low Span Gas to Air
  5. Click 2 to Set High Span Gas to CO2
  6. Set lower valve to the position CLEAN: rotameter flow increases to 2400 l/h and manometer pressure decreases to zero
  7. Click 3
    Click Graph Meas Data to see the graph
    Wait until the values are stable
  8. Click 4
  9. Click 5 and wait 300 s
  10. Click 6
  11. Click 7
  12. Turn off pump 1
  13. Set both valves to the position CLOSED
  14. Click 8
  15. Connect CO2 tube to the inlet
  16. Open CO2 bottle and set the manometer to 0.2 bar
  17. Set rotameter to 5 lpm
  18. Click 9
  19. Click 10 and wait 300 s
  20. Click 11
  21. Click 12
  22. Check on the calibration constants (take a photo for elog)
  23. Click 13
    Write calibration label as hh:mm dd month yyyy COVILHA SN70844204 AIR/CO2
    Click OK
  24. Click 14
  25. Put CO2 bottle manometer to zero
  26. Close the CO2 bottle
  27. Disconnect the CO2 tube from the neph
  28. Connect the instrument tube inside
  29. Set the upper valve to OPEN and the lower valve to CLEAN
  30. Turn on pump 1
  31. Click 15 and wait until the zero measurement ends (the sound of the motor will be heard)
  32. Exit Calibration and close the NephWin Software
  33. Set the lower valve to OPEN
