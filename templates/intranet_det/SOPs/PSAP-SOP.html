PSAP SOP
Version 1: 2015.10.12
Changing filters
  1. Desktop: on konsole, stop PSAPacquire script:
    Ctrl+c
  2. Laptop: on nephelometer software:
    Click Stop Data button
  3. Rack: on the PSAP instrument:
    Turn power switch off
    Open the filter holder and discard the old filters to the box labeled DISCARDED FILTERS
    Install new filters in holder: sample side of the filter up
    Check that all 4 O-rings are in the optic unit (above and below holder) and haven't fallen out
    Turn power switch on
    Check rotameter flow is 90 l/h and manometer pressure is near -0.2 bar
    Hold reset switch up for 30 s
  4. Laptop: on nephelometer software:
    Click Start Data button
    Verify the suggested name is ok and click Open button
    Neph Status should show a green button and say Okay
    Next Data in... secs should start a countdown
  5. Desktop: on konsole, restart PSAPacquire script:
    ./PSAPacquire
  6. Desktop: on elog take note of the filter change just done
    Click New
    Author: your username
    Type: select Routine
    Category: select PSAP
    Subject: Filter change
    Click Submit
    Create a new event for any other occurrence you found during the procedure.
